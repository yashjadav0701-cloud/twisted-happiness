    Return Policy

    Because of the significant personal effort and artistic dedication required, all sales are final. We strictly prohibit returns, exchanges, or refunds for "change of mind."

    Transit Damage Exception: If your item arrives structurally damaged due to courier mishandling:

    1. Record a continuous, unedited unboxing video starting from the sealed package.
    2. Contact us via WhatsApp within 24 hours of delivery.

    Upon verification, our artisan will review the claim and issue a replacement. Cash refunds are not provided.

    Privacy Policy

    1. Information Collection: We collect necessary fulfillment data: your full name, shipping address, email, and phone number.

    2. Logistics: Your shipping details are securely transmitted to Shiprocket solely for generating manifests and tracking.

    3. Payment Security: We operate on a Zero-Data Payment architecture. We do not collect banking details. All transactions occur securely within your UPI application.

    4. Data Protection: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party data brokers.
